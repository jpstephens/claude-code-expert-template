# Decision Log

This document records significant decisions made during the project. Each decision follows the ADR (Architecture Decision Record) pattern.

---

## Template

```markdown
### Decision [NUMBER]: [TITLE]

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded  
**Decision Makers:** [Which experts were involved]

#### Context
What is the situation? Why are we making this decision now?

#### Decision
What have we decided to do?

#### Alternatives Considered
1. **Alternative A:** Description
   - Pros: ...
   - Cons: ...
   - Why not: ...

2. **Alternative B:** Description
   - Pros: ...
   - Cons: ...
   - Why not: ...

#### Consequences
- **Positive:** What good things result from this decision?
- **Negative:** What tradeoffs are we accepting?
- **Risks:** What could go wrong? How do we mitigate?

#### Expert Perspectives
- **Product (Sarah):** [Input]
- **Engineering (Chris):** [Input]
- **Security (Alex):** [Input]
- [Other relevant experts]
```
